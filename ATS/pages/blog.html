High Tea at Baur au Lac

High Tea, 20.February 2022

The seasonal expirience, only for a few cold winter months.
Baur au Lac will serve you a neat variety of teas, in fact as many as you may want to drink during your stay there. The High Tea or Afternoon Tea as they call it, includes the traditional Etagere with 3 tiers. On the very bottom one you will be delighted to find some savory sandwiches, on the second tier are the classic scones and finishing of on the top tier some sweet patisserie.
